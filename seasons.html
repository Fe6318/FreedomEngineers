2021 - Infinite Recharge at Home
Due to the Covid-19 pandemic, there were no in-person competitions. Instead, each team did challenges at home
We were one of few teams that were able to build a new robot during the pandemic. This robot had the ability to shoot dodgeball sized balls or "power-cells".
The at home competitions consisted of driving obstable courses and shooting challenges. Apart from the at home challenges, in June 2021, we competed at an unofficial competition in Manitowac, Wi. At the competition we placed second at made it to the finals.
